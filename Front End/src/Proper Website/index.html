About — us

Electrofusion is all about bringing students together to create an unforgettable experience. We hold our events at the coolest venues and bring in the best brands and bands to give college students the opportunity to explore their cities and their new found freedom. It’s everything you’re looking for, right in one place. Keep checking back, Electrofusion expands around the country. Or sign up to become a Electrofusion Insider, to ensure you’re always in the know.








Upcoming Events

Coding Contests

Ready to solve fun, challenging problems? Kick Start hosts online rounds throughout the year, giving participants the opportunity to test and grow their coding abilities while getting a sample of the programming skills needed for a technical career. Participate in one or join them all!

Technical Model

The Technical Model Contest is an effort to bring together students across the nation with distinguished dignitaries to provide the view of the potential of the research works being carried out. It is aimed to create an opportunity for collaboration to conceive and execute new ideas!

Research Paper

The paper contest is divided into six parallel tracks. Prizes will be awarded for the best papers in each technical track. The number of papers in each track / each domain may vary. The length of the paper should not exceed 6 pages. The language of the manuscript should be in English.

E - Sports

Esports tournaments are computer game competitions between two teams of 5–6 players. Teams duel each other in popular games like League of Legends (LoL), Overwatch and Fortnite to determine the winner. If you perform well at a tournament you may even be offered an esports scholarship.
